## Event Overview

On November 15, 2025, the Rotary Club of Manila held its annual Community Food Drive, bringing together members and volunteers to support families in need during the holiday season.

## Planning and Execution

### Pre-Event Preparation
- Coordinated with 15 local businesses for donation collection points
- Recruited and trained 75 volunteers
- Secured refrigerated storage for perishable items
- Created distribution schedule for maximum efficiency

### The Drive Day
The event kicked off at 6 AM with volunteers setting up distribution centers across three locations. By 2 PM, we had:

- Collected **2 tons** of non-perishable food
- Received $5,000 in monetary donations
- Assembled 500+ food packages
- Distributed to families across 12 barangays

## Lessons Learned

1. **Early planning is crucial**: We started organizing 2 months in advance
2. **Community partnerships matter**: Local businesses were instrumental
3. **Clear communication**: Daily briefings kept everyone aligned
4. **Flexibility**: Weather changes required quick adaptations

## Next Steps

Building on this success, we're planning:
- Monthly mini food drives
- A permanent donation collection center
- Youth engagement program to involve younger community members
- Partnership with 5 additional organizations for wider reach
